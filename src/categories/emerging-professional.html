Emerging Professional

Eligibility

We will accept nominations from any individual meeting the following criteria:

  • Employed and based in the West Midlands region
  • Under 35 years of age as of 1 April 2014
  • Category sponsor employees are not eligible for this award

Judging Criteria

The judges will pay particular attention to:

  • A significant track record of outstanding achievement
  • Entrepreneurial innovation and flair
  • Providing selfless support to charity or other good causes
  • Promoting the region
  • Industry recognition, accreditation and accolades
  • Commitment to customer service, supported by testimonials

General

All financial information will be treated in the strictest of confidence and will not be disclosed to anyone other than the panel of judges and adviser's. Please provide enough relevant information to enable the assessors to form a clear picture of your achievements.

Entry Submission

All submissions to be received by 5pm Thursday, 31 July 2014
